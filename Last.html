Description:


The Last of Us is a highly acclaimed action-adventure game developed by Naughty Dog. Set in a post-apocalyptic world devastated by a fungal infection, players follow the journey of Joel, a hardened survivor, and Ellie, a young girl with a mysterious immunity to the infection. Together, they must traverse dangerous environments, scavenge for resources, and navigate through hostile human and infected encounters.

The game offers a gripping and emotionally charged narrative that explores themes of survival, loyalty, and the complexities of human relationships in desperate circumstances. The bond that forms between Joel and Ellie serves as the heart of the story, as they face unimaginable challenges and make difficult choices in their quest for safety and a potential cure

With a combination of stealth, strategic combat, and exploration, players must navigate through beautifully rendered environments, both urban and natural, as they unravel the secrets of this post-pandemic world. The gameplay mechanics emphasize resource management, crafting, and intense encounters with both hostile human factions and deadly infected creatures that lurk in the shadows.

The Last of Us delivers a cinematic experience, with stunning visuals, immersive sound design, and nuanced performances by its cast. The game's rich storytelling, character development, and morally ambiguous choices have resonated with players and critics alike, earning it widespread acclaim and numerous awards.

The Last of Us stands as a testament to the power of storytelling in video games, offering a deeply emotional and gripping journey through a hauntingly beautiful and dangerous world.

System Requirements:


To fully enjoy The Game The Last of Us Part-1 and ensure smooth gameplay, your gaming system should meet the following recommended system requirements:


Minimum Requirements:

  • Operating System: Windows 10 (Version 1909 or Newer)
  • Processor: AMD Ryzen 5 1500X, Intel Core i7-4770K
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: AMD Radeon RX 470 (4 GB), AMD Radeon RX 6500 XT (4 GB), NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970 (4 GB), NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1050 Ti (4 GB)
  • Storage: 100 GB available space


Recommended Requirements:

  • Operating System: Windows 10 (Version 1909 or Newer)
  • Processor: IAMD Ryzen 5 3600X, Intel Core i7-8700
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: AMD Radeon RX 5700 XT (8 GB), AMD Radeon RX 6600 XT (8 GB), NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2070 SUPER (8 GB), NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3060 (8 GB)
  • Storage: 100 GB available space


Please note that these system requirements are for the original release of The Last Of Us Part-1 on PC. It may be possible to run it on lower-end hardware or higher-end hardware not listed here. However, for the best experience, it is recommended to meet or exceed the recommended system requirements.
